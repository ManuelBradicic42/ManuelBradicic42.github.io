I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Machine Learning at KTH University in Sweden. My academic background includes graduating with high honors in BSc (Hons) Computer Science from the University of Surrey in the United Kingdom. My academic journey reflects a strong dedication and interest for expanding my expertise in developing and applying computer vision and signal processing techniques to the field of biology. Alongside my academic pursuits, I manage a part-time job to support my studies.I am actively involved in sports, excelling in rowing with a national title in the UK in 2021, practicing Jiu-Jitsu, running, and continuously learning languages.

I am also conducting an internship at Karolinska Institutet in Carlen's lab within the Department of Neuroscience where I work on the signals collected from medial prefrontal cortex of living mice using silicon probes.
